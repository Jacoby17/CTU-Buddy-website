About Us

CTU-Buddy is an educational system developed by a team of passionate students and educators at City Technical University.

Our Vision

To enhance the learning experience and academic success of CTU students by providing an interactive and user-friendly platform.

Our Mission

To create a collaborative learning environment where students can easily manage their timetables, engage in discussions, and share educational resources.

Hello, I'm Jacobus Spreeth, the driving force behind CTU-Buddy.
